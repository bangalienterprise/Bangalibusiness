# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Bangali Enterprise! Follow this guide to get up and running in minutes.

## 1. Login with Demo Accounts
No signup required! We have pre-seeded accounts for every business type.

1.  Go to the **Login Page**.
2.  Use **Email:** `retailowner@bangali.com` and **Password:** `Password123!` to see the **Retail** dashboard.
3.  Use **Email:** `agencyowner@bangali.com` to see the **Agency** dashboard.

## 2. Create a New Business
1.  Click **Sign Up** on the login page.
2.  Fill in your details.
3.  **Step 3:** Select your business type (e.g., "Education").
4.  Complete setup. You will be redirected to your new Education Dashboard.

## 3. Join an Existing Business
Simulate adding a team member:
1.  Login as **Retail Owner**.
2.  Go to **Team Management** (Sidebar).
3.  Click **Invite Member**. Select "Seller" role.
4.  Copy the generated code (e.g., `RET-1234`).
5.  Logout.
6.  Go to `/join-business`.
7.  Enter the code.
8.  Follow prompts to create an account or login. You are now a Seller in that business!

## 4. Explore Features
-   **Retail:** Go to POS, click items to add to cart, click "Checkout" to record a sale. Check Dashboard to see revenue update.
-   **Agency:** Go to Projects, move a project card from "To Do" to "Done".
-   **Service:** Go to Calendar, click a slot to book a mock appointment.

## 5. Admin Powers
1.  Login as `admin@bangali.com`.
2.  Go to **Global Dashboard**.
3.  See system-wide stats.
4.  Go to **User Management** to suspend/ban users.
